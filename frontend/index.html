Step 01 — Retrieve

Vector search across your medical library

Semantic embeddings map your question to the most relevant passages in ChromaDB.

Step 02 — Grade

Each chunk scored by an LLM judge

Irrelevant context is discarded before it can pollute the answer. Only high-confidence passages proceed.

Step 03 — Correct

Web fallback when confidence is low

Tavily's real-time search is triggered automatically, grounding answers in current literature.

Step 04 — Generate

Grounded answer with full attribution

Groq LPU synthesizes a cited response with confidence score, source list, and audit trail.
